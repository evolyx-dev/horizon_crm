## 1. Welcome

Welcome to Horizon CRM's Customer Portal! This is your personal space to:

- **Submit travel inquiries** — tell us where you want to go
- **Track your inquiries** — see the status of your requests  
- **View bookings** — access your confirmed travel details
- **See itineraries** — view your day-by-day travel plan
- **Track payments** — check payment status and balances
- **Provide feedback** — rate your travel experience

---

## 2. Accessing the Portal

### Logging In

1. Open your browser and go to your travel agency's portal URL
   - Example: `https://youragency.example.com/portal`
2. Enter your **Email** and **Password**
3. Click **Login**

### First-Time Setup

If this is your first visit:

1. Your travel agent will provide your login email and a temporary password
2. Log in with the temporary password
3. You'll be prompted to set a new password
4. Fill in your profile details (name, phone, address)

### Forgot Password?

1. On the login page, click **Forgot Password**
2. Enter your email address
3. Check your inbox for a password reset link
4. Click the link and set a new password

## 4. Submitting a Travel Inquiry

### How to Submit an Inquiry

1. Click **New Inquiry** or **Submit Inquiry** on the portal
2. Fill in the inquiry form:

| Field | Description | Example |
|-------|-------------|---------|
| **Destination** | Where you want to go | Bali, Indonesia |
| **Travel Type** | Type of trip | Beach / Adventure / Honeymoon |
| **Departure Date** | When you want to leave | 2026-06-15 |
| **Return Date** | When you want to return | 2026-06-22 |
| **Number of Travelers** | How many people | 2 |
| **Budget Range** | Your approximate budget | ₹1,50,000 - ₹2,00,000 |
| **Special Requirements** | Any special needs | Vegetarian meals, wheelchair accessible |
| **Additional Notes** | Anything else | "Celebrating our anniversary, would like a sunset dinner" |

3. Click **Submit**

### What Happens Next

After you submit:

1. **Confirmation**: You'll receive an email confirming your inquiry
2. **Assignment**: A travel agent from the agency will be assigned to your inquiry
3. **Contact**: The agent will reach out to discuss your requirements (usually within 24 hours)
4. **Quote**: You'll receive a proposed itinerary and price quote
5. **Confirmation**: If you accept, the inquiry becomes a confirmed booking

### Tips for Better Inquiries

- **Be specific about dates**: Even approximate dates help agents find the best deals
- **Mention preferences**: Hotel style, activity level, dietary needs
- **State your budget**: This helps agents tailor proposals to your range
- **List all travelers**: Include ages of children if applicable

## 5. Tracking Your Inquiries

### Inquiry Status

Your inquiries go through these stages:

| Status | What It Means |
|--------|---------------|
| 🆕 **New** | Submitted — waiting for agent assignment |
| 📞 **Contacted** | An agent has reached out to you |
| 💰 **Quoted** | A price quote and itinerary have been sent to you |
| ✅ **Won** | You've accepted — booking is being created |
| ❌ **Lost** | Inquiry was closed (cancelled or expired) |

### Viewing Inquiry Details

1. Navigate to **My Inquiries** in the portal menu
2. Click on any inquiry to see full details
3. The detail page shows:
   - Your travel details (destination, dates, travelers)
   - Current status with progress indicator
   - Communication history with your agent
   - Attached documents (itinerary PDF, etc.)

## 6. Viewing Your Bookings

### Booking Status

Once your inquiry is confirmed, it becomes a booking:

| Status | What It Means |
|--------|---------------|
| ✅ **Confirmed** | Trip is booked and confirmed |
| ✈️ **In Progress** | You're currently traveling |
| 🏁 **Completed** | Trip is finished |
| ❌ **Cancelled** | Booking was cancelled |

### Booking Details

Your booking page shows:

- **Trip Summary**: Destination, dates, number of travelers
- **Financial Summary**: Total cost, amount paid, balance due
- **Linked Itinerary**: Click to view your day-by-day plan
- **Payment History**: Record of all payments made
- **Agent Details**: Your assigned travel agent's contact info

## 7. Viewing Your Itinerary

### Day-by-Day Plan

Your itinerary is a detailed day-by-day plan for your trip:

| Day | Information Included |
|-----|---------------------|
| Day 1 | Activities, hotel check-in, airport transfers |
| Day 2 | Sightseeing, meals, transport arrangements |
| ... | Each day is detailed with timings and locations |
| Last Day | Check-out, departure transfer, flight details |

### What's Included

For each day, you'll see:
- **Activities**: What you'll be doing (tours, excursions, free time)
- **Accommodation**: Hotel name and room type
- **Transport**: How you'll get around (transfers, flights, car rental)
- **Notes**: Special instructions, meeting points, tips
- **Cost Breakdown**: Cost for that day's activities

### Downloading Your Itinerary

- **PDF Export**: Click "Print" or "Download PDF" to save a copy
- **Email**: Ask your agent to email the itinerary to you
- **Mobile Access**: The portal is mobile-friendly — access your itinerary on your phone during travel

## 8. Payment Information

### Payment Schedule

Your booking will have a payment schedule. Common patterns:

| Payment | When | Typical Amount |
|---------|------|---------------|
| **Advance** | At booking confirmation | 25-50% of total |
| **Second Installment** | 30 days before departure | 25-50% of total |
| **Final Payment** | 7-14 days before departure | Remaining balance |

### Payment Methods

Accepted payment methods vary by agency but commonly include:
- Bank Transfer / NEFT / RTGS
- Credit Card / Debit Card
- UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm)
- Cash (at the agency office)

### Checking Payment Status

1. Go to **My Bookings** in the portal
2. Open your booking
3. The **Financial Summary** section shows:
   - **Total Amount**: Full trip cost
   - **Paid Amount**: What you've paid so far
   - **Balance Due**: What's remaining

## 9. Providing Feedback

After your trip is completed, we'd love to hear about your experience!

### Submitting Feedback

1. Navigate to **Feedback** in the portal menu (or click the feedback link in your completed booking)
2. Select the **Booking** you're reviewing
3. Rate your experience:
   - ⭐ to ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ (1 to 5 stars)
4. Write your **Comments**:
   - What did you enjoy most?
   - Was anything below expectations?
   - Would you recommend this trip to others?
5. Click **Submit**

### Why Feedback Matters

- Your feedback helps the agency improve their services
- It helps other travelers make informed decisions
- Constructive feedback on specific details (hotel quality, guide knowledge, transport comfort) is most valuable

## 11. FAQ

### Q: How quickly will I hear back after submitting an inquiry?
**A**: Most agencies respond within 24 hours during business days. For urgent requests, call the agency directly.

### Q: Can I modify my inquiry after submitting?
**A**: Yes, you can add notes to your inquiry or contact your agent directly. For major changes (dates, destination), it may be better to submit a new inquiry.

### Q: How do I know if my payment was received?
**A**: Check the payment history in your booking. Payments are updated once the agency records them. If a payment isn't reflected within 2 business days, contact your agent.

### Q: Can I cancel a booking?
**A**: Yes, but cancellation policies vary. Contact your travel agent as soon as possible. Cancellation fees may apply depending on how close you are to the departure date.

### Q: I can't log in. What should I do?
**A**: Try the **Forgot Password** link first. If that doesn't work, contact your travel agency directly — they can reset your account.

### Q: Can I book for someone else?
**A**: Yes. When submitting an inquiry, specify the names and details of all travelers. The booking will be under your account but list all travelers.

### Q: Is my personal data safe?
**A**: Yes. Your data is stored securely in your agency's dedicated database. Each agency's data is completely isolated. The platform follows standard security practices including encrypted connections and secure password storage.

### Q: How do I get a receipt for my payment?
**A**: Your booking page includes the payment history. You can also ask your agent to send a formal invoice or receipt via email.
